THE HARMONY SYNAPSES


Where Notes Become Connections, and Music Unites Hearts.

Harmony Synapses is a club dedicated to bringing together musicians and music enthusiasts who share a passion for creating, performing, and enjoying music. Whether you're an experienced instrumentalist, vocalist, or simply someone who appreciates a good melody, this club provides a space for like-minded individuals to connect, collaborate, and celebrate the joy of music.

Achievements

These achievements provide a roadmap for the club's progress and contribute to its overall success and impact within the campus community.
Musical Workshops

Conduct a series of successful workshops on various musical topics, such as songwriting, music production, or instrument techniques. Due to the large variety of musicians in the club, workshops have been held for several different instruments and genre ranging from classic pop and rock to traditional sitar arrangements.

Local Gig

Secure a performance opportunity at a local venue, showcasing the talent within the club. The club has been invited several times to local venues for hour long performances

Music Charity Event

Organize and host a charity event or performance to support a musical or community-related cause. The club has successful raised money for associations like the Immune Deficiency Foundation and the Deaf Senior Citizens Support Center. .

Music Festivals

Represent the club by participating in local or regional music festivals. The club has successful won first price in the HornBill festival(2023). The club also recently performed at the NH7 Weekender festival(2023)
