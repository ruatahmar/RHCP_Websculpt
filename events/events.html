Weekly Open Mic Nights

Step into the spotlight and unleash your creativity at The Harmony Synapses' Weekly Open Mic Nights! Every Fridays, our stage is yours to share your talents, whether you're a seasoned performer or trying it out for the first time. From captivating acoustic sets to electrifying spoken word performances, our open mic welcomes all forms of expression. Join us for a night of camaraderie, laughter, and unforgettable performances as we come together to celebrate the power of music and spoken word. Don't miss your chance to shine – sign up or simply come by to enjoy the show!

Date: Every Friday!

Time: 7:30 PM-9:30 PM

Venue: The Harmony Synapses Club Room

Participation criteria: Be a part of the HS community.

Spectators can walk in.

With marshmallows roasting and guitars strumming, it was the perfect opportunity for members to unwind, connect, and enjoy the simple pleasures of music and friendship under the starlit sky.
